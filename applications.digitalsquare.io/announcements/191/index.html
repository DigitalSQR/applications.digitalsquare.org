Request for Application #2019-020

Digital Financial Services on Health Outcomes and Health Systems

Modification to RFA #2019-020: Cancellation of live Q&A teleconference on November 4th, 2019

Submitted by Celia Lang (PATH) on Tue, 10/29/2019 - 13:02

RFA #2019-020 has been modified.The live Q&A teleconference call scheduled for November 4, 2019 has been cancelled. Digital Square will still be hosting the live Q&A webinar on Friday, November 8, to answer any questions regarding the application co-creation phases for this RFA. Like the previous webinars, this is a time for you to ask any questions about the technical scope or how to use the OAP. The Q&A webinar will take place at 10AM - 11AM Eastern Time on Zoom (https://path.zoom.us/j/579837071).  Digital Square will post the recording and transcript from the webinar to our wiki by November 8.
